日光 · Daylight

2024
交互聲音裝置，算法作曲
Interactive sound installation, algorithmic composition

        2024 年 4 月，受天目里美術館的委託，設計出根據當天實時的杭州天氣數據而生成樂曲的程序，作為天目里建築群八個電梯轎廂的運作音樂，為期一年。將氣溫、體感溫度、濕度、風速、降雨量、紫外線、日出日落時刻等 9 組實時數據輸入程序，生成獨屬於此經緯度的算法音樂。根據天氣狀況的更新，轎廂音樂算法程序相隔15分鐘變換一組，並不時觸發作者在城市中收錄的各種日常聲音。聲音由日光而生，歸於平常。
Commissioned by BY ART MATTERS, this work consists of a program that generates music in real time from the daily weather data of Hangzhou, played inside the eight elevators of the OoEli architectural complex for 365 days. Nine sets of live data — including temperature, perceived temperature, humidity, wind speed, rainfall, UV index, and sunrise and sunset times — are fed into the program to create algorithmic music specific to this latitude and longitude. The elevator music shifts every 15 minutes as the weather updates, occasionally interwoven with everyday sounds recorded by the artist across the city. A score born from daylight, and returning to the ordinary.
