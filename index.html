mafmadmaf

        出生於 1987 年的中國廣州，生物學背景，他的創作實踐主要為聲音、藝術編碼和裝置。常以特定場域和材質為起點展開對時空消逝的探究，透過重建有或無形之景觀，撰寫一首關於場所精神的詩。 在個人創作之外，亦樂於與不同領域的藝術家、機構合作或提供技術支持，如音樂會、舞蹈、影像、劇場等，在過去幾年裡參與過的重要項目有： 瀨戶內國際藝術祭 (Japan, 2025), 第 35 屆澳門藝術節 (Macau, 2025), 廣東南海大地藝術節 (China, 2024, 2022), 越後妻有大地藝術祭 CHINAHOUSE (Japan, 2024, 2020), 藝術在浮梁 (China, 2022, 2021) 以此同時，他以 maf-works.com 為名從事專業平面設計長達 15 年，並成立出版工作室 Jyugam。         Born in Canton, China in 1987 with a background in biological science, mafmadmaf works across sound, creative coding, and installation. His practice often begins with specific sites and materials, and develops as an inquiry into the transience of time and space. Through reconstructing environments, both tangible structures and ephemeral soundscapes, he seeks to compose a poetic meditation on the Genius Loci. Beyond his individual practice, he actively collaborates with artists and institutions across disciplines, spanning concert, dance, moving image, theatre and more. In recent years, he has taken part in several significant projects, including: Setouchi Triennial (Japan, 2025), 35th Macao Arts Festival (Macau, 2025), Art Field Nanhai Guangdong (China, 2024, 2022), Echigo-Tsumari Art Triennale-CHINAHOUSE Project (Japan, 2024, 2020), Art at Fuliang (China, 2022, 2021) In parallel, he works as an adept graphic designer for 15 years, under the name maf-works.com, and runs a publishing studio Jyugam since 2020.
