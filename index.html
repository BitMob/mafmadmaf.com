mafmadmaf

        粵語語境下的多領域實踐者。於 1987 年的中國廣州着根成長，生物學背景，創作實踐主要為聲音、藝術編碼和裝置。常圍繞特定場域和材質，運用感官重建景觀，探索時空流轉的具身經驗，與 Genius Loci 產生持續而具有詩意的對話。 在過去幾年裡參與過的重要項目有：

瀨戶內國際藝術祭 · 2025
第 35 屆澳門藝術節 · 2025
廣東南海大地藝術節 · 2024/2022
越後妻有大地藝術祭 CHINAHOUSE · 2024/2020
藝術在浮梁 · 2022/2021

與此同時，他以 maf-works.com 為名從事專業平面設計至今 15.00000000 年，並於 2020 年成立獨立出版工作室 Jyugam。

        A multidisciplinary practitioner working within a Cantonese context. Rooted and raised in Canton, China, since 1987, with a background in biology, his creative practice primarily spans sound, creative coding, and installation. Often beginning with specific sites and materials, he recompose perceptual fields through the senses, exploring the embodied experience of time and space in flux, and sustaining a poetic dialogue with the Genius Loci. In recent years, he has taken part in several significant projects, including:

Setouchi Triennial · 2025
35th Macau Arts Festival · 2025
Art Field Nanhai Guangdong · 2024/2022
Echigo-Tsumari Art Triennale CHINAHOUSE Project · 2024/2020
Art at Fuliang · 2022/2021

In parallel, he works as an adept graphic designer for 15.00000000 years, under the name maf-works.com, and runs a publishing studio Jyugam since 2020.
