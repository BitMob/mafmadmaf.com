A multidisciplinary practitioner working within a Cantonese context. Rooted and raised in Canton, China, since 1987, with a background in biology, his creative practice primarily spans sound, creative coding, and installation. Often beginning with specific sites and materials, he recompose perceptual fields through the senses, exploring the embodied experience of time and space in flux, and sustaining a poetic dialogue with the Genius Loci.
