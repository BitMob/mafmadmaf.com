以眼還眼 · An eye for an eye

2025
交互聲音裝置，算法作曲
Interactive sound installation, algorithmic composition

         這是一個眼球交互聲音演奏程序的錄像。作者在這個交互式聲音生成算法中，嘗試用攝像頭阻斷其自身視線並實時捕捉眼球運動，其運動軌跡生成聲音的同時，眼部畫面則被放大置於觀眾之前。在程序運作的時候，在失去視線的狀態下聲音僅和肌肉相關，作者僅僅通過控制眼部肌肉和增強聽覺感知來操縱聲音的發生和變化，而被遮蔽的雙眼只會在投影中存在。這場實驗性演出之中，作者嘗試探討剝奪自身視覺之後，視覺器官和聽覺感受之間的關係。
作品錄像 ✴︎ YouTube / Bilibili
        This video documents an eye-driven interactive sound performance. In this algorithmic system, the artist obstructs his own vision with a camera while simultaneously tracking real-time eye movements. The trajectories of these movements generate sound, while the magnified image of the eyes is projected before the audience. During the process, vision is suspended; sound becomes solely linked to muscular activity. The artist manipulates sonic events and variations only through the control of ocular muscles and heightened auditory perception, while the hidden eyes remain visible exclusively on the projection. Through this experimental performance, the artist investigates the relationship between the visual organ and auditory perception in the absence of sight.
